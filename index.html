Know us

Mantilam provides leading-edge IT services, outsourcing and business solutions to organizations worldwide enhancing business certainty, competitive advantage and innovation.

For around two decades, Mantilam Solutions is helping enterprises to stay steadfast in a volatile business environment through its comprehensive portfolio of IT and IT-enabled services with a distinct focus on offshore software development, application management, product engineering, BPO and mobility services.

Started as an offshore software development company, today Mantilam has established footprints in global markets with technology partnerships, worldwide customers and company marketing & sales offices. Mantilam capabilities have successfully extended to dedicated practice assemblies for Microsoft Dynamics, Mobilization, Cloud Computing and for Insurance and Publishing Industries.
